VEGETARIAN:

A vegetarian diet excludes meat, poultry, and fish, focusing instead on plant-based foods. Vegetarians may consume dairy and eggs, depending on their specific dietary choices. This lifestyle is often adopted for health, ethical, or environmental reasons. A well-planned vegetarian diet can be very nutritious, rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. It can also be more sustainable and compassionate towards animals.
